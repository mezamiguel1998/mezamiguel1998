Background

Marcus Mason was a 29-year-old man who was found dead in the alley behind his apartment building near 11th and Xenia in Denver on April 8, 2006. He was a father of three and had no criminal history. His case remains unsolved 15 years later.

Details

There have been no known suspects in Marcus Mason's murder case. Despite interviewing witnesses, friends, and family, the Denver Police Department has not been able to find any leads or persons of interest in the case.

The Denver Police Department has continued investigating Marcus Mason's murder, even 15 years after the crime took place.
