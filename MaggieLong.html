Maggie Long Cold Case

Background

Maggie Long, a 17-year-old high school student, disappeared on December 1, 2017, in Bailey, Colorado. Her remains were found inside her family's burned home. The case remains unsolved, and the Park County Sheriff's Office continues to investigate.

Investigation and Timeline

  • December 1, 2017: Maggie Long disappeared.
  • December 4, 2017: Authorities announced that Long's remains were found in the burned home.
  • January 30, 2018: Park County Sheriff's Office released composite sketches of three potential suspects.
  • December 2018: Investigators announced that the suspects likely targeted the Long family home intentionally.
  • 2018-2022: The investigation continues without any significant breaks in the case.
  • 2022: The Long family and local community organize a memorial to honor Maggie Long's memory.
